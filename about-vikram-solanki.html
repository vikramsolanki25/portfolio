Hi, I’m Vikram Solanki, a UI/UX designer from India.

An HFI Certified Usability Analyst, with 9+ years of industry experience, focused mainly on advocacy of the users. Pursued my engineering in Information Technology from Mumbai University, so it’s Engineer turned Designer, and it packs me up with a bit more enthusiasm and understanding about the technology.
